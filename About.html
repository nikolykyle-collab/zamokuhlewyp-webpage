About Us

Zamokuhle Women and Youth Projects (ZWYP) is a non-profit organisation formed in 2004 by women volunteering to provide services to vulnerable groups through donations of time, food, clothes, and other essential amenities.

This service expanded across districts to include food gardens, agricultural projects such as chicken farming, youth enterprise development, business management, and support services.

Our current focus is a rural empowerment project addressing economic, health, and social challenges. ZWYP collaborates with civic organisations, government agencies, and private sectors to maximize impact and benefit the communities we serve.
