Zamokuhle Women and Youth Projects also known as

ZWYP

ZWYP works with other civic organisations, government departments and agencies as well as private sectors to maximize impact, synergy and collaboration for the benefit of the communities we identify to serve.

Vision Statement

ZWYP will be a high-quality provider of a passionate and caring service, based on the needs of the individuals, families and communities driven by advocacy for humanitarian needs. We will contribute as individuals and families to a healthy community that ensures our safety and security.

Mission

Our mission is to enable the provision of care and support to those in need, based on quality, passion and advocacy for human rights. We aim to conduct research to identify sources of discomfort and work together to find amicable relief or solutions on a sustainable basis. We endeavor to enable the provision of awareness, training and capacity building for effective results.
